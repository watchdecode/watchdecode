## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Which G-Shock is best for daily use in India under ₹15,000?**
A: The GA-B2100 is the best all-round daily G-Shock under
₹15,000. Its slim profile, solar charging, and analogue-digital
display make it practical for every situation from office
wear to outdoor activities. If budget is tighter, the
GW-M5610U at ₹9,995 offers solar charging and atomic
timekeeping that the GA-B2100 lacks.

**Q: Does atomic timekeeping work on G-Shock watches in India?**
A: The Multiband 6 atomic radio signal does not function
in India as Casio does not have a local transmitter station.
However, the watch keeps accurate quartz time independently
and can be synced manually or via third-party mobile apps
like Clock Wave on iOS. Bluetooth-equipped models like the
GBD-200 and GA-B2100 sync time automatically through
the Casio app.

**Q: Is the GA-B2100 resin version safe to buy in India's climate?**
A: The standard GA-B2100 with resin bezel has been reported
by some owners to experience minor deformation of the resin
around the case in extreme heat conditions over extended
periods. If this concerns you, the GM-B2100 with metal
bezel eliminates this issue entirely, though it costs
significantly more. For most Indian climates and normal
daily use, the standard GA-B2100 performs reliably.

**Q: Which G-Shock has the longest battery life?**
A: Any G-Shock with Tough Solar charging — the GA-B2100,
GW-M5610U, G-5600UE, and Rangeman — charges from any
light source and never requires battery replacement under
normal use. Among battery-powered models, the GBD-200
lasts approximately 3 years on a single charge, which
is above average for G-Shock digital models.

**Q: Where is the best place to buy G-Shock in India?**
A: Casio India's official website and authorised retail
stores provide full warranty coverage. Amazon India and
Flipkart also stock genuine G-Shock models through Casio's
official seller account — always verify seller identity
before purchasing. Grey market imports are generally
genuine but carry no Indian warranty and may lack local
service support.
