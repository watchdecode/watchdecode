## Boderry Voyager Titanium — The One Most People Should Buy

![Boderry Voyager Titanium](/images/posts/best-boderry-watches-2026/boderry-voyager.png)

image source- [www.boderry.com](http://www.boderry.com)

The Voyager is Boderry's flagship and the model that put them on
the map. It comes in both titanium and bronze variants — the
titanium is the better daily wearer, the bronze develops patina
over time if that appeals to you.

**Key Specs**

* Case: Titanium, 40mm
* Movement: NH35 automatic
* Crystal: Sapphire
* Water resistance: 100m
* Price: \~$159 USD

**What the community says:** Watch enthusiasts consistently
highlight how the Voyager punches well above its price. The
combination of titanium, sapphire crystal, and a reliable NH35
movement at this price point is genuinely hard to find elsewhere.
Lug-to-lug length is slightly long on smaller wrists — worth
checking before buying.

**Pros**

* Titanium case is feather-light and comfortable all day
* NH35 is a proven, serviceable movement
* Sapphire crystal resists scratches well
* 100m water resistance covers most daily activities

**Cons**

* Lug-to-lug length can feel long on wrists under 16cm
* Delivery from official site can take 3-4 weeks to India
* Limited authorised service options in India

**Verdict:** If you want your first automatic watch with serious
materials at an honest price, the Voyager is the answer. It
competes with watches costing three times as much.

* Movement: NH35 automatic
* Crystal: Sapphire

appreciate the bronze version for its character, while the titanium

* 200m water resistance — genuinely dive-capable
* Bronze version develops unique patina with wear
* Solid build quality with no reported finishing issues
* NH35 movement is easy to service anywhere in India

**Cons**

* Design is more niche than the versatile Voyager
* Bronze requires occasional cleaning to manage patina
* Slightly thicker profile than the Voyager

**Verdict:** If you want a diver under $200 with genuine
enthusiast credentials, the Sea Turtle delivers. The bronze
version especially is a conversation starter.

## Boderry Landmaster — The Field Watch With Serious Lume

![Boderry Landmaster](/images/posts/best-boderry-watches-2026/boderry-landmaster.png)

image source- [www.boderry.com](http://www.boderry.com)

The Landmaster is Boderry's most distinctive model. It uses
tritium-style glass tube lume technology alongside traditional
luminous markers, giving it a lume display that genuinely stands
out — especially in low light.

**Key Specs**

* Case: Titanium, 38mm
* Movement: NH35 automatic
* Crystal: Domed Sapphire
* Lume: Glass tube luminous technology
* Water resistance: 100m
* Price: \~$219 USD

**What the community says:** The Landmaster's tritium-style tubes
generate genuine excitement among watch enthusiasts who understand
how rare this feature is at this price. The 38mm size wears
elegantly and the domed sapphire crystal adds a vintage feel.

**Pros**

* Glass tube lume is genuinely impressive and long-lasting
* 38mm is a versatile size that suits most wrists
* Domed sapphire crystal looks premium
* Titanium bracelet is extremely lightweight

**Cons**

* Most expensive Boderry model
* 38mm may feel small for buyers used to larger watches
* Field watch aesthetic is not for everyone

**Verdict:** The Landmaster is for buyers who appreciate the finer
details — the lume technology alone justifies the price premium
over the Voyager for the right buyer.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Are Boderry watches available in India?**
A: Boderry does not have an official India distributor yet. Most
Indian buyers order directly from boderry.com or through grey
market importers. Expect 3-4 weeks delivery and factor in import
duties of approximately 20-25% on top of the listed price.

**Q: Is the NH35 movement reliable for daily use?**
A: Yes. The Seiko NH35 is one of the most widely used automatic
movements in the world. It runs at 21,600 bph, has a 41-hour
power reserve, and can be serviced by any watch technician in
India familiar with Seiko movements.

**Q: How does Boderry compare to Seiko or Orient at similar prices?**
A: Boderry's advantage is materials — titanium and sapphire at
these price points are rare even from Seiko and Orient. However,
Seiko and Orient have established India service networks, which
Boderry lacks. For pure specs per rupee, Boderry wins. For
after-sales peace of mind, Seiko wins.

**Q: Which Boderry watch is best for someone buying their first**
**automatic?**
A: The Voyager Titanium. It is the most versatile design, wears
comfortably, and the NH35 movement requires minimal attention.
It is also Boderry's most popular model, meaning community
support and owner advice is easiest to find.

**Q: Is Boderry's customer service reliable for Indian buyers?**
A: Mixed reports. Some buyers have had smooth experiences, while
others have faced delayed responses to shipping issues. Given the
lack of local support, buying through an established grey market
importer in India may be safer than ordering direct.

## Final Verdict

**Budget buyer (under ₹10,000):** Go with the Urban Titanium.
You get titanium, sapphire, and an NH35 at a price that beats
anything comparable from established brands.

**Value buyer (₹13,000–15,000):** The Voyager Titanium is the
clear recommendation. It is Boderry's best all-rounder and the
model that consistently earns the strongest community endorsement.

**Quality buyer (₹18,000+):** The Landmaster justifies its
premium with the glass tube lume technology and domed sapphire
crystal. If those details matter to you, it is worth every rupee.

Boderry is not perfect — customer service can be inconsistent and
India delivery is slow. But for the specs on offer at these
prices, they are hard to argue with.
